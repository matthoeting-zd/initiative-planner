Quadrant Definitions
Quick Wins High impact + High simplicity — Do these first.
Strategic Bets High impact + Low simplicity — Worth the effort.
Fill-Ins Low impact + High simplicity — Do when capacity allows.
Kill List Low impact + Low simplicity — Avoid or deprioritize.
Scoring Criteria (1–10)
  • Time to Value: How quickly will this deliver results?
  • Simplicity: How easy is it to implement?
  • Mission Return: How aligned is it with strategic goals?
  • Low Cost: How affordable is it?
FIT Score

Average of all four criteria (1–10). Higher = better overall fit.
